IndoStage Creative & Production Pvt. Ltd.
Where Indian Heritage Meets Global Stages
IndoStage Creative & Production Pvt. Ltd. is a premier cultural and entertainment company dedicated to presenting India’s rich artistic legacy to audiences across the world. We curate, create, and celebrate the finest in Classical Music, Indian Dance, Folk Art, World Music, and Concept-Based Performances, bringing timeless traditions into contemporary global arenas.
Our Vision
To become India’s leading cultural production house that connects heritage with innovation, nurtures emerging talent, and delivers world-class artistic experiences on national and international platforms.
What We Do
Classical Music & Dance
We present exceptional performances in:
Hindustani & Carnatic Classical Music
Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Odissi, Kuchipudi & other classical dance forms
Instrumental recitals, jugalbandis, and thematic classical productions
Our shows celebrate depth, purity, and the spiritual essence of Indian classical arts.
Folk Music & Dance
India’s vibrant folk heritage shines through our presentations of:
Lavani, Koli, Banjara, Gondhal, Powada
Regional folk music and traditional instruments
Tribal and rural art forms
We strongly believe in bringing regional and rural artists to the mainstream stage and showcasing the diversity of India’s cultural fabric.
Conceptual & Fusion Concerts
Innovative, thematic programs designed for modern audiences, such as:
Live Concert Series of Bollywood
Sitar Symphony & Indian–Western Fusion Ensembles
Jazz blends, all-instrumental shows, and youth-oriented creative experiments
These productions offer a refreshing mix of nostalgia, creativity, and global soundscapes.
Corporate & Public Events
We professionally organise:
Corporate shows
Public cultural festivals
Political and city-based promotional events
Heritage and historical theme events
With a strong execution team, we ensure seamless management, creativity, and audience connect.
Film & Media Production
Our production wing creates:
Documentaries
Short Films
Web Series
Music Albums
Artist Profiles & Promotional Films
We aim to tell stories rooted in culture, history, tradition, and human experiences.
Training & Workshops
We regularly conduct:
Acting Workshops
Dance & Classical Music Workshops
Folk Art Training
Instrumental Training Sessions
Future plans include full-scale reality shows, talent hunts, and artist development programmes.
Promoting New & Rural Talent
At the heart of IndioStage is our commitment to discovering, nurturing, and promoting talented artists from rural and underrepresented regions. We offer them a dignified platform, professional grooming, and opportunities to perform in national as well as international shows.
Indian culture grows when every artist is heard—and we are here to make their voice reach the world.
Our Global Aspiration
IndioStage aims to take Indian artistry beyond borders.
We aspire to collaborate with international festivals, cultural organisations, Indian embassies, tourism boards, and global corporate houses to create impactful, memorable experiences on global stages.
Our mission is to ensure that India’s music, dance, rhythm, and stories resonate across the world.
Why Choose IndoStage?
Strong artistic network across classical, folk, and contemporary fields
Professional production team with years of experience
Concept-driven, culturally rich programming
Global-ready performance formats
Commitment to quality, authenticity & innovation
